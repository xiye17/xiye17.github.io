I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin, advised by Greg Durrett.

My research interest focuses on Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I am currently working on Semantic Parsing with long and complex utterances.

Before coming to UT Austin, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the School of Software, Tsinghua University, where I worked with Shixia Liu in Visual Analysis and Machine Learning.
