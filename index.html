xi.ye [at] princeton.edu   xiye17   scholar   xiye_nlp

I am a Postdoctral Researcher at Princeton Language and Intelligence.

I study language models: how they reason, and how to enable deeper, more general, and more reliable thinking. My work spans post-training, inference techniques, and evaluation benchmarks. Lately, I'm especially interested in general-purpose reasoning models and long-context reasoning.

I obtained my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at UT Austin, advised by Greg Durrett. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the School of Software, Tsinghua University.
